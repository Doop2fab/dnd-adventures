Dench

Class: Fighter
Race: Half-Orc
Level: 6
Alignment: Chaotic Good
Played by: Joel

"The sea is freedom - freedom to go anywhere and do anything."

Bio / Backstory

Born to a human mother and an orc father, Dench hails from a tribe where both humans and orcs live in peace. However, not everyone was accepting of interspecies relationships. Dench left his parents to join a crew and explore the vast ocean. Throughout Dench's childhood, he worked under the command of several captains, some kinder than others, who taught him the intricacies of surviving through the bitter nautical conditions.

Shortly after reaching adulthood, Dench recieved word of a king who was trying to recruit adventurers throughout the land so as to retrieve a magical item - the icosahedron. Before venturing to this kingdom, he and his crew had one more stop to make - Dry River Port. While there, he encountered the first half-orc he had ever seen besides himself. She was an elegant woman named Misha. They instantly bonded, but Dench knew he couldn't stay in that port-town forever. Promising he would return once again, Dench set off for Neverwinter.

Details / Extras

He loves his greatsword and hates that he's 44 years old.

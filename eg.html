Eg

Class: Cleric
Race: Aarakocra
Level: 5
Alignment: Neutral Good
Played by: Jacob

"To live is to suffer"

Bio / Backstory

Once born a regular-ass bird, Eg was extremely charismatic and cool, even for birds. Everyone likes each other when you're a bird so he wasn't like a rock star or anything like that but rest assured he was all the good things you want to be and a bird. One day when he was flying along somewhere in the sky he got hit with an anthropomorphization beam and became a bird-person, instantaneously forcing into existence a bizarre race of people who are birds with a name quickly generated by the extremely fast stream of sounds and letters that flooded Eg's brain as it expanded to accommodate two extra limbs and some other stuff, a name which will not be replicated here for brevity. Regrettably, this name was quickly lost to time as all things are which are not written down, and so "Aarakocra" was settled for instead because of its similar bizarreness. At any rate, the resulting Aarakocra had no real place they existed in within the Material Plane and so the gods who were on shift that day frantically started choosing random locations to let the Aarakocra flow in from the Elemental Plane of Air, which exists as you can tell by its real-sounding name. Eg was already in a place when he became himself but the gods didn't recall placing him so they picked him up and placed him in a completely new area which he didn't recognize. Though many other Aarakocra were fortunate enough to have been placed in distances close enough to congregate in various bands, Eg was kind of just alone, which explains why he’s presently travelling with a bunch of sweaty weirdos who keep trying to kill themselves instead of chilling with some bangin hot hens or something.

Before that, though, he found his way to a village, where he was quickly taken in by the local lord as an oddity to deliver to the king in order to patch up a particularly regrettable faux pas a fortnight or so back. The lord didn’t feel especially fulfilled after having sent the strange birdperson off, but was glad to not have to deal with that weirdness anymore regardless.

As hindsight now informs us, Aarakocra freak the veritable fuck out when they’re confined, and so Eg’s short stint as a strange addition to the king’s throne room was quickly ended as the reality of the situation became apparent. A person-sized animal without the capacity to be trained against its naturally casually-defecative habits has the propensity to generate a mindboggling amount of liquid shit. The king, head aching from the inhuman squalling beating against his hungover skullmeat, ordered the birdthing to be released and “please oh gods do something useful just shut the hell up,” thus starting Eg’s quest to find something justifying his existence.
